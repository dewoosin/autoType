📋 How It Works

  • Step 1: Type any text (Korean, English, emojis, special characters)
  • Step 2: Text is automatically Base64 encoded (100% ASCII safe)
  • Step 3: ESP32 receives and transmits the Base64 string via HID
  • Step 4: PC1 receives the Base64 string
  • Step 5: Use the decoder to convert back to original text
  • Decoder Setup: Click "Send Base64 Decoder" once to setup PC1
